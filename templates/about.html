About AO3 Wrapped

Inspired by Spotify Wrapped, AO3 Wrapped generates user statistics based on your Archive of Our Own profile data. Here’s what AO3 Wrapped can show you:

• Total word count of fics read
• Top ships and fandoms
• Character that showed up most frequently
• Most common relationship category
• Age rating breakdown
• Word cloud of your most frequent tags
• The month you read the most (only available for yearly Wrapped)

FAQ

Why does AO3 Wrapped need my password?

AO3 wrapped cannot access the reading history pages necessary to generate your Wrapped without being logged in to the website. If you would prefer not to log in, you can generate a smaller Wrapped with your public bookmarks instead.

My stats don’t look right! What’s wrong?

AO3 Wrapped uses web scraping to gather reading information since Archive of Our Own does not currently have a public API.

This means that reading stats may not be 100% reflective of actual reading done on AO3 (for example, clicking on a work adds it to your history regardless of how long you stayed on the page). Your total word count may also be off if you read works written in languages that don’t use spaces, like Chinese and Japanese, because of the way AO3 calculates word counts.
